## Recommended Study Strategy

### ✅ Study Order

```
1. Technical Accounting & Reporting (Area II) — 35-45% weight
   → Start here — leverages your FAR knowledge while fresh
   
2. Business Analysis (Area I) — 40-50% weight
   → New material for most candidates; needs dedicated time
   
3. State & Local Governments (Area III) — 10-20% weight
   → Smallest weight; create a fund comparison table
```

### ✅ MCQ Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Total MCQs before exam | 1,000-1,500+ |
| Review every explanation | Even for questions you get right |
| Full practice exams | At least 2 under timed conditions |

### ✅ Key Study Tips

1. **Take BAR immediately after FAR** — massive content overlap
2. **Leverage your FAR notes** — don't re-study from scratch
3. **Practice data analytics** — interpreting charts, scatter plots, trend lines
4. **Create a fund comparison table** — measurement focus + basis of accounting
5. **Use spaced repetition** — review mnemonics daily (Anki works great)
6. **Focus on "why wrong"** — BAR has 2-3 close answer choices per MCQ
7. **Government accounting** — smallest area but easy points if studied
